Privacy Policy

Nuts & Bolts: Sorting Puzzle · Last updated: 7 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nuts & Bolts: Sorting Puzzle (the “Game,” “we,” “us”) handles information when you use our Android application. We respect your privacy and have built the Game to be playable fully offline. Please read this policy carefully.

Summary: The Game has no accounts, no login, and no servers of our own. All your game progress is stored only on your device. The only data leaving your device is handled by Google AdMob to show ads, which may use your advertising identifier.

1. Information We Collect

We (the developer) do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information ourselves. The Game does not ask you to sign in, register, or provide your name, email, phone number, or location.

Game data such as your level progress, stars, coins, settings, and unlocked themes is stored locally on your device only (using Android’s on-device storage) and is never sent to us.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob to keep the Game free. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain information, including:

  • Your device’s advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (such as device model, operating system version, and general region)
  • App interaction and ad-performance data
  • Approximate (non-precise) location derived from IP address

This information is used by Google to deliver and measure ads (including personalized ads where permitted) and to detect fraud and abuse. We do not control and are not responsible for Google’s data practices. Learn more in Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. Consent (EU/UK Users)

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the Game uses Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before personalized ads are shown, in accordance with applicable laws. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time, where this option is offered, from within the Game.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the advertising services, please contact us so it can be addressed.

5. Your Choices

  • Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Clear local game data at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the Game.
  • Adjust ad-consent preferences (where shown) from within the Game.

6. Data Security & Retention

Because we do not collect or store your data on any server, there is no personal data held by us to retain or delete. Local game data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall the Game.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of the Game after changes means you accept the revised policy.
